2009

LEMIFFE. DECEMBER 2025

2009 is an EP inspired by sketches I recorded during that year in Mexico and Scotland. The challenge for this month's EP was to record at least one track only using ukulele and vocals.

This is the last EP from project 12 so I decided to make it a bit of a trip down memory lane, considering the sketches were recorded back then (the first ones I have on video), so the topics and lyrics are about people I knew, places I would go to, and my move to Scotland that year.

I've also published a PDF (EP booklet) similar to last release where I include the lyrics, information and photos I took that year. You can find it on Gumroad. I hope you enjoy listening to it!

2019

LEMIFFE. APRIL 2025

2019 is an EP based on sketches I recorded in 2019 in Belgium, which is arguably the best year of my life; Having travelled to the US, Belgium, Denmark, Iceland and other places, running a marathon, amongst many other things, it was a year full of crazy high notes and a few shocking moments. I tried to capture some of that vibe in these 4 tracks.

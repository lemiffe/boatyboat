2018

LEMIFFE. JUNE 2025

2018 is an EP inspired by sketches I recorded in Belgium during that year. This month's EP challenge was the opposite of the last one: Instead of purely acoustic recordings I went for fully-electronic. Only samples, loops, VSTis, built-in instruments, not a single second of anything recorded on my microphone.

It ended up being a bit synth-waveish with a touch of NIN; a bit eerie, dark and intense, but following the melodies and harmonies of the sketches I recorded back in 2018. A bit different from my normal work, but that's the challenge: Working within constraints.

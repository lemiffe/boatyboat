FRUTAL, vol. 1

LEMIFFE. AUGUST 2024

FRUTAL is an album that combines silliness with seriousness, you try to read into the lyrics looking for something deeper but in the end it's just about fruits.

The style of the album is quite varied, from harder-hitting rock tracks like Cherries, to softer ones with a touch of folk and indie in tracks such as Apples and Buddha's Hand. There are also a few electronic tracks as well as a couple of interludes inspired by supermarket sounds and other shenanigans.

You can find the backstory for the album on Lemiffe's website.

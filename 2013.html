2013

LEMIFFE. MARCH 2025

2013 is an EP based on sketches I recorded in 2013 in Mexico during a very tumultuous year. Juggling owning a business, figuring out my future, dealing with massive amounts of stress, yet at the same time having on of the most epic years of my life, led to a lot of interesting and weird compositions during this time.
